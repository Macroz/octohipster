make-link-header
(make-link-header links)
Compiles a collection of links into the RFC 5988 format.
Links are required to be maps. The :href key going into the <> part.
eg. {:href "/hello" :rel "self" :title "Title"}
    -> </hello>; rel="self" title="Title"
